Papers

Virtualization

Papers on basic virtualization concepts:

  • Gerald J. Popek and Robert P. Goldberg. 1974. Formal requirements for virtualizable third generation architectures. Communications of the ACM 17, 7 (July 1974), 412-421.
  • Paul Barham, Boris Dragovic, Keir Fraser, Steven Hand, Tim Harris, Alex Ho, Rolf Neugebauer, Ian Pratt, and Andrew Warfield. 2003. Xen and the art of virtualization. In Proceedings of the nineteenth ACM symposium on Operating systems principles (SOSP '03). ACM, New York, NY, USA, 164-177.
  • Ulrich Drepper. 2008. The Cost of Virtualization. Queue 6, 1 (January 2008), 28-35.

Other interesting papers on virtualization:

  • Alex Ho, Michael Fetterman, Christopher Clark, Andrew Warfield, and Steven Hand. 2006. Practical taint-based protection using demand emulation. In Proceedings of the 1st ACM SIGOPS/EuroSys European Conference on Computer Systems 2006 (EuroSys '06). ACM, New York, NY, USA, 29-41.
  • Grzegorz Miłós, Derek G. Murray, Steven Hand, and Michael A. Fetterman. 2009. Satori: enlightened page sharing. In Proceedings of the 2009 conference on USENIX Annual technical conference (USENIX'09). USENIX Association, Berkeley, CA, USA.

Dynamic memory allocation

Papers on two-level segregated fit (TLSF), the memory allocator used in the hypervisor:

  • M. Masmano, I. Ripoll, A. Crespo, and J. Real. 2004. TLSF: A New Dynamic Memory Allocator for Real-Time Systems. In Proceedings of the 16th Euromicro Conference on Real-Time Systems (ECRTS '04). IEEE Computer Society, Washington, DC, USA, 79-86.
  • M. Masmano, I. Ripoll, J. Real, A. Crespo, and A. J. Wellings. 2008. Implementation of a constant-time dynamic storage allocator. Software—Practice & Experience 38, 10 (August 2008), 995-1026.
  • Miguel Masmano, Ismael Ripoll, Patricia Balbastre, and Alfons Crespo. 2008. A constant-time dynamic storage allocator for real-time systems. Real-Time Systems 40, 2 (November 2008), 149-179.
