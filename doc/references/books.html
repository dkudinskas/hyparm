Books

This page lists books relevant to gain a better understanding of OS and hypervisor concepts.

  • Silberschatz, A., Galvin, P., and Gagne, G. 2008 "Operating System Concepts". 8th. Wiley Publishing.
    Relevant chapters: 1, 2, 3, 8, 9, 13, 14, 15 and 19.
  • Smith, J. and Nair, R. 2005 "Virtual Machines: Versatile Platforms for Systems and Processes (The Morgan Kaufmann Series in Computer Architecture and Design)". Morgan Kaufmann Publishers Inc.
    Relevant chapters: 1, 2, 3, 4, 8, 10 and appendix A.
